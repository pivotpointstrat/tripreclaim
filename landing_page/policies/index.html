⚡ The 24-Hour Rule — Your Most Powerful Tool

Under DOT regulations, all US airlines must offer a full cash refund if you cancel within 24 hours of booking, regardless of fare type — as long as the flight is at least 7 days away.

If the price drops within 24 hours of booking, cancel and rebook at the lower fare for a free full refund.

✅ TripReclaim monitors your flights every 15 minutes during this 24-hour window — 4 checks per hour to catch every drop.
