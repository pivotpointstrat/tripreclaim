Price Drop Policy Overview

Turkish Airlines does not offer price-drop adjustments or price-match guarantees. After booking, options depend on fare class: Promotional/Discounted economy: non-refundable, non-changeable. Economy Restricted: changeable with fee. Economy Flexible: free changes, partial refund. Business Class Flexible: fully refundable. EU261 rights apply to TK flights from EU airports and to EU destinations operated by Turkish Airlines — relevant for schedule changes that may trigger refund rights.

✅ US DOT 24-Hour Cancellation Rule

For tickets originating in the United States, Turkish Airlines complies with US DOT 24-hour cancellation rule: full refund within 24 hours provided departure is at least 7 days away. Cancel via Manage My Booking at turkishairlines.com or by calling 1-800-874-8875. Note: Turkish Airlines hubs through Istanbul IST — US to IST routing is extremely popular and prices fluctuate regularly.

The 24-hour window is your most powerful tool. If you spot a lower fare within 24 hours of booking, you are entitled to a full cash refund — use it.

Basic Economy / Lowest Fare Rules

Turkish Airlines Promotional fares (lowest economy): strictly non-refundable and non-changeable after the 24-hour window — full forfeiture on cancellation. Economy Restricted: changeable with fee (typically $100–200 depending on route); non-refundable. Economy Flexible: free date and route changes; cancellation with partial refund minus fee. Business Flex: fully flexible, fully refundable. Miles&Smiles award tickets have separate rules.

⚠️ Always check your fare conditions before booking. Lowest-price fares often have zero flexibility after the 24-hour window.

Miles&Smiles Award Ticket Policy

Turkish Airlines Miles&Smiles award tickets: miles redeposited upon cancellation. Cancellation fee: approximately $50–100 USD per person. Classic Elite and Elite Plus members receive reduced fees. Cancel at least 24 hours before departure. Expired miles can sometimes be reinstated for a fee — contact Miles&Smiles customer service.

Step-by-Step: How to Claim a Price Drop Refund

  1. Step 1: Log in to turkishairlines.com and go to Manage Booking
  2. Step 2: Enter your PNR (booking code) and last name
  3. Step 3: Check your fare class — Promotional, Restricted, or Flexible determines your options
  4. Step 4: For Flexible fares: use the Change Flight option to rebook at the lower available fare
  5. Step 5: For Restricted fares: calculate if change fee + lower fare price represents net savings vs original ticket
  6. Step 6: For tickets within 24-hour US DOT window: cancel for full refund, then rebook at the lower price
  7. Step 7: For refund requests: submit at turkishairlines.com/en-us/any-questions/refund-form/
  8. Step 8: Call Turkish Airlines US at 1-800-874-8875 for assistance

💡 Insider Tips & Loopholes

  • EU261 rights apply on Turkish Airlines flights departing from EU airports or arriving in EU on TK metal — €250–600 per person for 3+ hour delays or cancellations
  • Istanbul IST is Turkish Airlines hub — connections through IST to Asia, Africa, and Middle East are often the cheapest routing and prices drop frequently
  • Turkish Airlines schedule changes of 3+ hours entitle you to a full refund on any fare class — monitor your booking for schedule updates in the weeks before departure
  • US DOT 24-hour window applies for all US-originating TK tickets — JFK/IAD/ORD/LAX/SFO to IST routes see frequent flash sales
  • Miles&Smiles miles can be earned by flying any Star Alliance partner — useful for building status toward elite benefits including reduced cancel fees
  • Book directly at turkishairlines.com for simplest access to change/cancel — OTA bookings add complexity to refund processing
