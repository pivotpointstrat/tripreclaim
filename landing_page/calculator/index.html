How Price Drop Probability Works

Our estimate is based on three key factors airline pricing researchers track

📅

Days Until Departure

Flights booked 30–90 days out see the most price volatility. Airlines reprice frequently to fill seats, creating drop opportunities — especially at night.

🌍

Route Type

International and transatlantic routes see larger absolute drops. Domestic routes drop more frequently but in smaller amounts. Both are worth monitoring.

⚖️

Ticket Price Tier

Higher-priced tickets tend to see larger absolute drops. A $800 fare has more room to fall than a $200 fare — both in dollars and percentage terms.
